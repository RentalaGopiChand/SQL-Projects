# Business Insights

## Key Findings

1. Total Revenue Generated: $1.26 Million

2. QUICK was the highest revenue generating customer.

3. Côte de Blaye was the highest revenue generating product.

4. Beverages generated the highest category revenue.

5. The company served customers across 21 countries.

6. Peak revenue occurred during April.

7. The top 10 customers contributed a significant share of total revenue.

8. Revenue was concentrated among a few high-value products.

9. Several customers placed orders in every year available in the database.

10. Window functions helped identify cumulative revenue growth over time.
